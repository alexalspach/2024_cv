Snapbot: A Reconfigurable Legged Robot
Kim, J., Alspach, A., Yamane, K.
Design and Hardware/Electronics Development, IROS 2017, Vancouver, Canada, Sept. 2017, Disney Research

Developed experimental hardware for Snapbot, a reconfigurable legged robot, to emulate natural configuration changes and various styles of legged locomotion.
