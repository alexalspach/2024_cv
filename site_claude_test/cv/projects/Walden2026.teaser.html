Walden Robotics: Building useful, general-purpose robots

Lead robot character and industrial design. I also build capable compliant end effectors and I lead the team that designed, built and maintains our first deployed robot fleet.

I'm excited to finally share what we've been working on at Walden Robotics.

Over twenty years now in robotics, and my motivation hasn't changed: Build robots that people want around, robots that help us, work alongside us, and amplify us. Robots that unburden us and enable us to focus on what makes us happiest and thrive. I am lucky to have the best team of creative and dedicated people to build these with! We are deploying in the real world and making a real impact, learning hard lessons with positive, constructive, and inspiring feedback coming our way every day.
