Compliant Fingers for Robotic Manipulation Capabilities Development

Developed 3D printed compliant fingers based on fin rays with rigid fingertips for fine manipulation, as well as other end effector hardware including depth camera mounts and capacitive fingers. Also helped design and build test kitchen for development and demonstration of our manipulation capabiltiies.

A later version, designed specifically for gripping and peeling potatoes, can now be seen all over the place! Open-sourced and popularized by TRI and Stanford's Universal Manipulation Interface (UMI), my design can be seen manipulating in academia and even being sold by a couple companies. 🥔
