A Humanoid Robot Pushing Model Inspired by Human Motion
Master's Thesis, Drexel University, Philadelphia, PA, June. 2012.

Human pushing analyzed using motion capture, along with forces exerted at the hands and feet, to determine the effect of stance length and body angle on pushing force. Insight gained used to create pushing controller for the humanoid robot HUBO.
