Darwin-OP Drives
Alspach, A., Mason, S., Sun, J., Oh, P.Y.,
Presentation for the Darwin-OP Humanoid Application Challenge, ICRA 2012, St. Paul, MN, May 2012.

Designed and fabricated iRobot Create-based car for Darwin-OP. Supported the development of line following algorithm as well as Darwin's steering and accelerating motions.
