Audrey III - Halloween Party Robot Speed Project

Envisioned, designed and built in day. Using a green balaclava borrowed from my neighbor, a 3D printed flower borrowed from my wife, a small robot arm borrowed from work, and a flower pot borrowed from my garden, I set out to build an animated flower that chaperoned our Halloween party, seemingly stared down the guests, and intimidated those that just wanted to grab candy from the pot. The little trick-or-treaters loved it too!
